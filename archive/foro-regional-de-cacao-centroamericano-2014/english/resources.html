Resources

For more than a decade, Lutheran World Relief has worked with smallholder cocoa producers in Latin America, the Caribbean and Asia to increase incomes and improve livelihoods by optimizing opportunities presented in the cocoa markets. LWR uses a climate-smart agricultural value chain approach to improve access to inputs, technical resources and capacity, post harvesting processing and export market access. Learn more.
