General Info

The Central America Regional Cocoa Forum brings together industry representatives, cocoa farmers, producer organizations, government ministries, research institutions, non-profit development actors and many other representatives from across the Central America cocoa value chain for discussions on issues of importance to the cocoa community.

Note: The Central America Regional Cocoa Forum will be conducted in Spanish.
